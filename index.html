Fire

It's Q1 of 2023. You're the senior HR lead. Leadership has misplaced most of their formal directives for you. All you know is everyone in the company - including you - has been put into a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP). Everyone is either Highly Valued (HV) or Least Effective (LE). You need to process as many employees as possible before the all staff begins.

In 20 seconds.


Your only guidance is a hasty Slack message from your skip level: retain HIGHLY productive workers and keep the LEAST disruptive employees, but lose the WORSENING profit centers and fire the MOST unhappy people.


That's.. great. Really great.
